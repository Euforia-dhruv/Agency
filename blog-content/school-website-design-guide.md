Your school website is often the first interaction parents have with your institution. A modern, well-designed website builds trust, streamlines enrollment, and keeps parents engaged.

This guide covers everything you need to know about school website design in 2025.

## Why Your School Website Matters

Parents research schools online before ever visiting in person. Your website needs to:

- Establish credibility within seconds
- Showcase your campus, faculty, and programs
- Simplify the admissions process
- Keep current parents informed
- Rank on Google for local searches

A poorly designed website can cost you enrollments. A great one becomes your most effective marketing asset.

## Essential Features for School Websites

### 1. Admissions & Enrollment Management

The number one feature parents look for. Include:

- Online application forms
- Document upload (report cards, ID proofs)
- Application status tracking
- Fee payment integration
- Scholarship/financial aid information

### 2. Parent Portal

Give parents real-time access to:

- Attendance records
- Grade reports
- Homework assignments
- Teacher communication
- Event calendars

### 3. LMS Integration

Connect your website with your learning management system (Moodle, Google Classroom, Blackboard):

- Single sign-on for students and parents
- Course materials access
- Assignment submissions
- Progress tracking

### 4. Event Calendar

A dynamic calendar with:

- Academic term dates
- Exam schedules
- Sports events and competitions
- Parent-teacher meeting dates
- Holiday schedules

### 5. Virtual Tour

Give prospective families a feel for your campus:

- 360° virtual tour
- Photo galleries by facility (labs, library, sports grounds)
- Video testimonials from students and parents

### 6. News & Blog

Share updates regularly:

- Principal's messages
- Student achievements
- Upcoming events
- Educational resources
- Alumni news

## School Website Design Best Practices

### Mobile-First Design

Over 70% of parents browse school websites on mobile. Your site must work flawlessly on phones and tablets.

### Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

School websites must be accessible to everyone:

- Proper heading hierarchy
- Alt text for all images
- High-contrast color ratios
- Keyboard navigation
- Screen reader compatible

### Fast Load Times

Parents expect pages to load in under 2 seconds. Optimize images, use a CDN, and choose a performant tech stack.

### Clear Navigation

Parents should find what they need in 2 clicks or less:

- Admissions info
- Academics
- Faculty
- Events
- Contact

## School Website Platforms

### Custom Built (Next.js)

Best for: Schools that need performance, SEO, and custom features

- 95+ Lighthouse score
- Full design control
- Scalable for growth
- Headless CMS for easy updates

### WordPress

Best for: Schools on a tight budget who need basic functionality

- Lower upfront cost
- Plugin-dependent
- Slower performance
- Security maintenance required
