Your restaurant website should do more than show your menu. The best restaurant websites drive online orders, fill reservations, and bring diners through the door.

## Must-Have Features

### 1. Online Ordering System

The single most important feature. Allow customers to:

- Browse your full menu with photos
- Customize orders (add-ons, special instructions)
- Choose pickup or delivery
- Pay online (cards, UPI, wallets)
- Track order status in real time

### 2. Table Reservation System

Integrate with platforms like Zomato, or build a custom booking flow:

- Real-time table availability
- Party size selection
- Special occasion notes
- Instant confirmation via email/SMS

### 3. Dynamic Menu Management

Update your menu in real time — no developer needed:

- Daily specials
- Seasonal menus
- Price changes
- Dietary filters (vegan, gluten-free)
- Availability status (sold out)

### 4. Photo Gallery

High-quality food photography converts browsers into diners:

- Professional food shots
- Interior and ambiance photos
- Event and private dining setups
- Behind-the-scenes kitchen content

### 5. Google Maps & Local SEO

Make it easy for customers to find you:

- Google Maps integration
- Directions and parking info
- Business hours and holiday schedules
- Google reviews widget
